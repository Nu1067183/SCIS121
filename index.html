Yummy Pasta with Tomato Sauce

h1>

Recipe by Zehra Nuri

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz (225g) pasta (e.g., spaghetti, penne, or your favorite)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (14 oz) can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for serving)

    • Instructions:

    1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
    2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    3. Add the chopped onion and sauté for about 3-4 minutes, or until it becomes translucent.
    4. Add the minced garlic and sauté for another 30 seconds until fragrant.
    5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens.
    6. Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning if needed.
    7. Serve the cooked pasta with the tomato sauce on top. Optionally, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese for added flavor.

    This pasta recipe is a personal favorite because it's quick, easy to make, and bursting with delicious flavors.
